Target Audience

The Target Audience will be those who live in the Bighorn Basin area of Wyoming that are looking to know the current status on the community of Cowley Wyoming.

Persona | Lisa

Lisa is interested in the daily activities that her kids will be involved with. She also would like to know if there are any activities that she can particiapte in with her husband Dexter.

Persona | Coach Simmons

Coach Simmons is looking forward to the youth basketball camp put on by the town and would like his basketball team to be involved to get more support from the community.

Scenarios

1. Community Swing Dancing

2. Youth Basketball Camp

3. Cowley Corn Cookout

4. Pioneer Day Parade

5. Labor Day 5k Marathon
