Target Audience

The Target Audience will be those who live in the Bighorn Basin area of Wyoming that are looking to know the current status on the weather in the area. This could include farmers, hunter, or just your everyday citizen in the area which would include the general public.

Persona | Hunter

In Wyoming, Hunting is a huge source revenue for the State and especially in the Bighorn Basin where hunting for Elk, Deer, Antelope, Bear, and many other game animals. As a result incoming hunters want to know the weather so that they can plan accordingly for their trip.

Persona | Farmer

Farming is a huge part of Wyoming's revenue and not only that is considered by many one of the most common livelihoods for Wyoming residents. Farmer's are very particular about the schedule that they follow for crops and need to always know what the weather is looking like.

Scenarios

1. Any incoming Harsh Weather?

2. How long will the Harsh weather last?

3. What is the weather projection like this weekend?

4. When should we expect rain?

5. When will the heat end?
