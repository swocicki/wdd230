Name

I chose Bighorn Basin Weather report because that is the area that I am from, and think it would be cool to design a website to fit the demographic that I understand most!

URL/Domain Name

The URL or Domain name for the website would be BBWeather.com

The Goal

The Goal for this website would be to provide the citizens of the Bighorn Basin in Wyoming with a reliable website that would update as quickly as possible to warn of the ever-changing weather in Northern Wyoming.
